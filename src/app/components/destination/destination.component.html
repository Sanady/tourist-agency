Accommodation

Sunny Days Palacio ****

Sunny Days Palacio is located in the northern part of Hurghada, next to the turquoise blue waters of the Red Sea, which surrounds the hotel from the east and south by a lagoon that sets within the hotel complex. In addition to the lagoon, the hotel has a small beach where the water is shallow due to the high tides and it is recommended to wear water sneakers. It is close to a coral reef, so the hotel is ideal for diving. It is 3 km from Sakala and 10 km from the airport.

Website of the hotel: https://www.sunnydaysegypt.com/index.php?lang=en
